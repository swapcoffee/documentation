## 🚀 1. Connect Your Wallet

To get started, you need to connect your wallet to the platform. It’s very simple:

1. Click the **"Connect Wallet"** button at the top right corner of the screen.
2. In the pop-up window, confirm the connection through your wallet.

---

## 💱 2. Select a Token to Swap

Once your wallet is successfully connected, you can select a token to swap:

1. Click the **"Select Token"** button.
2. In the list that appears, find the token you need (for convenience, use the search bar).
3. Click on the selected token.

> 💡 **Tip**: If the token you need is not in the list, you can import it manually by providing its **contract address**.

---

## ✏️ 3. Enter the Amount and Confirm the Swap

Now that the token is selected:

1. Enter the amount you wish to swap.
2. Carefully check the **swap details**.
3. Click the **"Swap"** button to confirm.

> ⚠️ **Important**: A transaction fee in **TON** is required for blockchain transactions. Make sure you have enough funds in your balance to cover it.

---

## ✅ 4. Confirm the Transaction

After clicking the **"Swap"** button:

- A request will appear in your wallet to confirm the transaction.
- Carefully review the details and confirm it.

---

## 🎉 5. Complete the Swap

Congratulations! 🎊 Your transaction has been sent to the blockchain. Now, you need to wait a bit:

- The system will process your transaction (usually takes **a few minutes**).
- After processing, you will see a notification of a **successful swap**.

---

Now you know how easy and fast it is to swap tokens on **swap.coffee**. Happy swapping! 🚀
