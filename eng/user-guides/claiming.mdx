## ❓ What is the Claim Center?

## 🛠️ How to Use the Claim Center?

We made the process as convenient as possible:

1. 🔗 Go to the [Claim Center](https://swap.coffee/claim).
2. 🔒 Connect your wallet to the platform.
3. 🪙 **Select Rewards**:
    - Click **"Select All"** for quick selection of all rewards.
    - Or choose specific tokens based on your preferences.
4. ✅ Click **"Claim All"** — and your rewards are on their way to you.
5. 📲 Confirm the transaction in your wallet.
6. 🎉 Once confirmed, enjoy your rewards!

> **Important**:
> We value your time and money. **swap.coffee** does not charge any additional fees for claiming rewards.
> You only pay the standard network fee.
