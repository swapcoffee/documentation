## 🛠️ How to Start Using the Referral System?

1. Go to [swap.coffee/referral](https://swap.coffee/referral).
2. **Connect your wallet**.
3. Find the button **"Get Referral Link"** or QR code.
4. Send the link or QR code to your friend.
5. When your friend clicks the link and connects their wallet, they automatically become your referral.

> 💡 **Tip**: The sooner you start sharing the link, the sooner you start earning!
